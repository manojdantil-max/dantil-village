Shri Mansa Mata Mandir sits majestically on a hilltop and is the most revered temple of Dantil. Devotees from nearby villages visit this temple especially during Navratri.
🌿 About Dantil Village

Dantil is a vibrant and peaceful village with a rich cultural heritage. Known for its warm community spirit, ancient temples, fertile farmlands, and the majestic Mansa Mata hilltop temple, Dantil is a place where tradition meets progress. The Grampanchayat works tirelessly for the development and welfare of every resident.
